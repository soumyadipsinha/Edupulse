ABOUT YOUR CONTEST

Choose Your Contest Platform:

Select a contest of your choice from the available options. Each contest has its own entry fee and prize pool. You can choose from various categories, such as Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, or Specialized contests.

Payment Mood On:

After selecting your contest, you'll be required to pay the entry fee before participating. Payment methods include credit/debit cards, wallets, or other available payment systems. Note: Your payment is processed securely. Once successful, you will be enrolled in the contest.

Performance-Based Rewards:

Based on your performance in the contest, you will receive double the entry fee back as a reward. The top performers will get higher payouts, while others will still be rewarded based on their rankings and the difficulty of their submissions. Example: If you paid $10 to enter and performed well, you could win up to $20 in prize money, depending on your ranking.
